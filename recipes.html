Strawberry Mango Banana Smoothie

Makes 1 serving.

Ingredients

  • 5 whole frozen strawberries
  • 3 pieces frozen mango
  • 1/3 whole banana
  • 1/3 cup soy milk
  • 1/2 piece date (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp ground flaxmeal

Instructions

  1. Add soy milk to blender.
  2. All all other ingredients to blender.
  3. Start blending on slow speed to break up ingredients.
  4. Increase speed gradually throughout blending. Blend until smooth.
  5. Pour drink and enjoy!
