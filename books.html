The Song of Achilles

by Madeline Miller

March 28, 2024 🌷

Link to Goodreads

Rating

5 stars out of 5

I have read this book twice before. Everytime, I am entertained. The novel follows Patroclus as he starts his new life in Phthia after a tragedy. He is demoted from the title of prince and forced to train for the King of Phthia's army. Yet, his fate brightens when he meets and forms a deep relationship with the Prince of Phthia, Achilles. Madeline Miller is a fantastic storyteller. Each chapter reads fluidly - I always find myself having a difficult time putting down this book!
